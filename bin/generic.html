Rara Base To Rara Lake

Option 1 by AIR

Day 01

 Flight from Kathmandu to Nepalgunj and then guest are Transfered to Banke Gabhar valley Home stay (Rara Base). After refreshing for some time local sight seen is done over night at Gabhar valley home stay 

Day 02

After Breakfast Drive to Airport for Flight to Talcha airport. Then after the arrival 2-3 hrs walking to Rara lake before night at Danfe Resort

Day 03

After Breakfast visit heaven of world Rara lake and spend night at Danfe Resort

Day 04

After breakfast transfer to airport for flight to Nepalgunj and Last flight back to Kathmandu. And Drive to sweet Home

Tentative price Rs.38, 000/- to Rs.40, 000/- per head 

Option 2 Jeep service, Bus service and Flight from Talcha to Nepalgunj

Day 01

Day Bus or Night Bus Journey from Kathmandu to Nepalgunj and Transfer to Banke Gabhar valley Home stay (RARA BASE) after some refresh time local sight is seen in dawn at Gabhar valley home stay 

Day 02

Early in the morning Drive to Kalikot via surkhet and dailekh and spend the night at Hotel madan or White House.

Day 03

Early in the morning Drive to Rara via Jumla and Mugu and spend night at Danfe Resort

Day 04

After Breakfast Visit Rara lake and spend night at Danfe Resort

Day 05

Early in The morning Guests are transfered to Nepalgunj through flight and then night journey to Kathmandu 

Tentative price Rs.22, 500/- to Rs.25, 000/- per head Minimum Group size 06 Required

Option 3 By Jeep and Bus

Day 01:

Day Bus or Night Bus Journey from Kathmandu to Nepalgunj and then guests are taken to Banke Gabhar valley Home stay (RARA BASE) and after some refreshment guest are taken to see local sight and spend night at Gabhar valley home stay

Day 02: Guests are taken to see the Local Sight and Drive to Dailekh via Surkhet and spend night at Dailekh Hotel park Inn or Hotel Panchkosi.

Day 03:

Early in the morning guest are taken to show the Local Sight and Drive to Jumla via Kalikot and spend night at Jumla Hotel Kanjirowa or Hotel Mansarowar or Hotel Amar Sandesh.

Day 04: Early in the morning Local Sight seeing at Jumla and drive to Rara Lake and spend night at Hotel Danfe.

Day 05:

After Breakfast Visit Haven of World Rara Lake and overnight at Hotel Danfe.

Day 06:

Early in the morning return back Kalikot and spend night at Hotel Madan or White House.   Kalikot

Day 07:

Early in the morning drive to Surkhet and spend night at Hotel Sidhartha or Hotel Namaste Nepal or Hotel Ashoka  Surkhet 

Day 08:

Drive back to Nepalgunj and from Night Journey travel  back to Kathmandu 

Tentative cost: Rs.28, 000/- to Rs.30, 000/- Minimum Group size 06 person required

  2. Breakfast ,Lunch and dinner everyday as per choice of the guest  
  3. Local Transportation

  2. Bar Bill and mineral water
