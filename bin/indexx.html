An Overview

3 days ago by Sajal Pandey

Nepal Highland Treks structure uncommon visit to Mustang for Nepali. Tour to Upper Mustang is experience of Trans Himalaya of Nepal bordering Tibet. Visit panoramic mountain views surroundings and scenic Plateau landscape. Upper Mustang is also called mini Tibet as it has the same landscape, people and their culture are same as of Tibetan People. Mustang Tour Cost for Nepali is depending how you want it. We can manage it as per your interest, time and budget.

Mustang has 2 sections
Lower Mustang begins from Beni and goes upto Kagbeni and Muktinath. It is also inside area of Annapurna conservation. No entrance fee is required For Nepali. They can reach there by private vehicle, local transportation or taking flight to Jomsom from Pokhara air terminal and get local transportation, Or visit there through trekking. But famous natural hot spring Tatopani cannot be missed., Unique village Marpha, Kagbeni and Muktinath Temple cannot be missed as well.

Kagbeni is gateway of Upper Mustang. It is in restricted area and special permit is required for foreigners other than Nepali. Nepalese do not need any entrance fee or permit. But some monastries and caves in Upper Mustang may ask for entrace fee or permit. You can reach Upper Mustang through Jeep, Cycle, and Motorbike or by Trekking.

Best ways to reach Mustang:
Mustang trip through motorbike: There is rough road connecting Lower Mustang and Upper Mustang from Pokhara alongside Beni. It reaches up to Lo- Manthang of Upper Mustang.

Mustang Trip through Jeep: Need four wheel jeep either for Lower Mustang or for Upper Mustang. Roads from Beni till Tibet boarder of Upper Mustang are all rough and dusty. Nepal Highland Treks can manage good ad comfortable Jeep for you Upper Mustang Jeep Tour either from all the way to Kathmandu or from Pokhara. There is no regular transportation that bring people from Jomsom to Upper Mustang there is no regular transportation.

Mustang Trip through Cycle: Since there is rough road in entire Mustang, reaching Lower and Upper Mustang is fun by cycling. It is long day with full on adventure.

Accommodation and foods in Mustang: Accommodation throughout the trip is through local tea house or Guest house. Local people of these areas have built their own house as guest house for all the trekkers and other visitors as well. It is most important that you eat your foods where you are accommodating. Room cost is not very much expensive; it can be from three hundred Nepali rupees to one thousand Nepali rupees for a room. Dometory rooms are also available for groups. You can select your food items from menues that are provided in the guest house.

Best Season to visit Mustang: We can visit Lower Mustang throughout the year although it might be difficult to visit in rainy season since the road migh be flooded in Kaligandaki. In rainy season flight to Jomsom from Pokhara may be affected. So the best season to visit Lower Mustang part is from March to May and September to November. January and February months are very suitable to Visit Lower Mustang. The best season to visit Upper Mustang is from March to November. Upper Mustang belongs to rain shadow area of Nepal. Less rain fall at raining season make this area little green which makes area more charming.

Mustang Trip cost for Nepali: It depends on your budget. Except all the transportation you might spend around 1,500 Nepali rupees per day that includes 3 times foods and accommodation. Beside that if you drink mineral water that may cost more. There is also option to drink spring water from local taps and River. If you want to take reserve Jeeps either from Kathmandu or from Pokhara please contact us.

Packing List It depends on type of your tour. You need more warm clothes than light clothes. Upper mustang might reach minus during the night. Warm blanket is provided from local guest house. If you bring warm sleeping bag yourself that would be better in Upper Mustang part. Up to Lower Mustang trip sleeping bag is not needed as they are provided in guest house.
